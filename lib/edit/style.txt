#W:class:level
#S:styles
#B:benefit

#An explanation of weapon styles and benefits.
#(If no style is listed for a given benefit,
# everybody enjoys the benefit, regardless of style chosen or exercised.
# This is caught by "w_info[i].styles==0" in the code).

#!!!All below should be somehow moved to help files and removed from here!!!

#NONE: No style. Limited benefits, but available regardless 
#  of weapon wielded, etc.
#UNARMED: Not wielding a weapon or shield.
#ONE-HANDED: Wielding 1 weapon and no shield. 
#  Prefers light weapons, and nothing over 20 lbs.
#TWO-HANDED: Wielding 1 weapon and no shield. 
#  Prefers heavy weapons, and nothing less than 10 lbs.
#WEAPON&SHIELD: Wielding 1 weapon and shield.
#TWO-WEAPON: Wielding 2 weapons, one in place of shield. 
#  Not all combinations work; try it for yourself.
#  With two weapons, if players get more than one attack,
#  2nd weapon gets every 2nd attack. Also, when charging,
#  players with two equipped weapons always get two attacks, instead of one.
#THROWN: Throwing (not firing) an item.
#SLING: Firing with a sling.
#BOW: Firing with a bow.
#XBOW: Firing with a crossbow.
#BACKSTAB: Wielding SWORD weapon against sleeping opponent.
#MAGIC_BOOK: Casting a spell from a magic book.
#PRAYER_BOOK: Casting a spell from a prayer book.
#SONG_BOOK: Casting a spell from a song book.
#INSTRUMENT: Wielding an instrument.
#RING, AMULET: Wearing a ring or amulet, respectively. Note that they can
#  be worn also in off-hand with these styles, but having them only 
#  in the off-hand does not count as an excercise of the style.
#POTION: Abilities related to potion-use, whether quaffing, picking up, 
#  throwing, etc. 
#SCROLL: Abilities related to scroll-use, such as reading and identifying them.

#The benefits are granted only when fighting in the particular style preferred.

#HIT: Gets bonus of (level-level required to get this ability)/2 to hit.
#  Note that "W:0:10 S:NONE B:HIT" works for melee, launchers and thrown.
#DAM: Gets bonus of (level-level required to get this ability)/2 to damage.
#AC: Gets bonus of (level-level required to get this ability)/2 to ac.
#XTRA_BLOW: Additional blow.
#XTRA_SHOT: Additional shot. With "S:NONE" works for both launchers and thrown.
#XTRA_MIGHT: Additional multiple on damage when fired/thrown.
#CRITICAL: Additional critical damage. With "S:NONE" works for melee, launchers and thrown.
#POWER: Gets bonus of 10 levels to effective spell level when computing damage, radius
# etc. and learn spells at 80% of the listed level requirement.
#ICKY_HANDS: Gets spell casting penalty when wearing non-free action gloves.
#  A Priest specializing in a single Magic Book
#  will be penalized with ICKY_HANDS for all his prayers, too.
#ICKY_WIELD: Gets spell casting penalty when wielding non-blessed weapon.
#BLESSED: Does not get spell casting penalty from icky wield.
#HOLD_SPELL: Can continue to cast spell for no energy cost while doing other
#things.
#RES_FEAR: Resistance to fear.
#ID: Automatically id basic type.
