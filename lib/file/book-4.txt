
                        Intermediate Theology
                        ---------------------

Herein is presented a carefully compiled list of benefits for medium-level
disciples of the 16 elemental Gods controlling the pits of Angband:
Note that the entry-level benefits still apply.

Chernobog, the God of Night -- Sustained dexterity.
Belobog, the God of Day     -- Holy aura of light.
Melkor, the God of Shadow   -- Resistance to darkness.
Svarog, the God of Fire     -- A more dextrous body.
Chthon, the God of Blood    -- Permanent heroism.
Mokosh, the God of Nature   -- See invisible.
Naunet, the God of Water    -- N/A
Voltumna, the God of Stone  -- N/A
Shaitan, the God of Evil    -- See invisible and telepathy.
Amon-Ras, the God of Good   -- N/A
Apep, the God of Chaos      -- A healthier body.
Radogast, the God of Sun    -- Resistance to bright light.
Herovit, the God of Rage    -- A stronger and more dextrous body.
Veles, the God of Beasts    -- Regeneration.
Stribog, the God of Winds   -- A more dextrous body.
Chenti, the God of Spheres  -- A more intelligent and wiser mind.
