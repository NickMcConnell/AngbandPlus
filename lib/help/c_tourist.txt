#####R=== Tourists ===

#####RWARNING! This is intended to be a chalange class, and as such
#####Ris more difficult than the others. It is not recommended for begginers.


#####GDescription
Tourists are strange being on vacation from the turmoils of life in 
Lower Earth. They are unprepared for the harsh realties of Middle-Earth
Tourists learn a few skills, but are bad at them, and can not worship
the ‘foreign devil-gods’, or use ‘foreign magic wish-wash’, except for
20c magic wishing stones bought from a worn out shop that are 
’guaranteed to work!’
