#####R=== Paladins ===

#####GDescription
A Paladin is a warrior/priest. They are very good fighters, but not 
very good at missile weapons. They receive prayers at a slower pace 
than priests, but can receive even the most powerful prayers. They are 
poor at  stealth, perception, searching, and magical devices. They have
a decent saving throw due to divine protection. 

#####GPrimary Stats
Strength
Wisdom (spell casting stat)

#####GMagic Usage
Paladins are holy warriors, and as such they may choose one devotion 
from among *****m_nether.txt*0[Nether], *****m_valar.txt*0[Valarin], and *****m_crusad.txt*0[Crusade]. 

The Nether Paladin is one of sinister aspect, wielding power over the 
undead. His counterpart, the Valarin Paladin, focuses on harnessing the
powers of good to aid him. The Crusade Paladin is a more warlike 
fighter, using his spells to enhance his skill in battle. Paladins may 
learn up to 32 spell levels.

#####GStarting Equipment
A paladin begins the game with:
    L1 spellbook of chosen realm
    A broadsword
    A scroll of protection from evil
If they are *very* lucky they may also begin with:
    a scroll of *enchant weapon*
