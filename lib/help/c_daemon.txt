#####R=== Daemonologists ===

#####GDescription
Adepts of the Chaos and masters of the Demons, members of this class 
can gain tremendous power over the demonkind. They also get their 
spells from their weapons, Demon Blades, which when wielded allow the 
Daemonologist to cast spells much faster. While when not wielded they 
cast MUCH slower.

#####GPrimary Stats
Intelligence (spellcasting stat)

#####GMagic Usage
Daemonologists are trained in *****m_chaos.txt*0[Chaos] and *****m_daemon.txt*0[Daemon] magic, and they can 
learn up to 64 spell levels total from these two realms.

#####GStarting Equipment
A daemonologist begins the game with:
    L1 book of chaos magic
    L1 book of daemon magic
    a scroll of word of recall
If they are *very* lucky they may also begin with:
    scrolls of enchant weapon to-dam
